Welcome!

2020-07-01

Welcome to this site. You will eventually see that I have interests that span many areas including:

  • Philosophy – of knowledge, of objects, of persons – and ethics and ultimately the various manifestations of spirit in the world

  • Mathematical, probabilistic and statistical modeling and computing

  • Art – its practice, influences, history

  • Linguistics – especially indo-european and semitic languages

  • Anything else – as all knowledge is fair game

Together we will traverse these domains, marvel at their connectivity, and I hope together discover new insights in the decisions we all make.

The now current handle on this site is a very short quote from Vergil Aeneid 4 varium et mutabile semper especially in regards to the topics here. While everything seems to be on the move, we should be always be aware, and undertand, a call to action.
